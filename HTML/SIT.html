Message from Director

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you and to introduce you to the ingenious world of Nanhi Pari Seemant Engineering Institute (NPSEI), Pithoragarh. N.P.S.E.I, Pithoragarh is one of the campus institutes of Veer Madho Singh Bhandari Uttarakhand Technical University, Dehradun, established in 2011 by the Govt. of Uttarakhand to promote technical education in Seemant areas of the state. Our objective is to prepare leaders, who go beyond conventional technical skills. Seemant Institute of Technology is a developing high-quality institute of international standards to meet the needs of the fast-changing 21st-century world.

Prof. (Dr.) Ajit Singh

Director, NPSEI, Pithoragarh
