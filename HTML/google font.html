Smart learning is a broad term for education in today’s digital age. It reflects how advanced technologies are enabling learners to digest knowledge and skills more effectively, efficiently and conveniently. Although the ongoing global movement towards smart learning marks an important paradigm shift in modern learning, many people still find the concept vague. It is best viewed from the perspective of the Three T’s, or Total Transformational Thinking.Total Transformational Thinking involves four components: learner, faculty, curriculum and the learning environment.
